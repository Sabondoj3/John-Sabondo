Our Goals

We are driven by a clear set of goals to help us achieve our mission and create a lasting impact. Here are the goals that guide our efforts:

Empowering Communities

Our goal is to empower vulnerable communities by providing resources and support for self-sufficiency. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive.

Promoting Education

We aim to provide educational opportunities to children and young adults, ensuring they have the skills and knowledge to succeed in their personal and professional lives.

Strengthening Families

Our focus is to rebuild and strengthen family structures, providing support to single mothers, orphans, and families in need of assistance.

Skill Development

We aim to provide training and development opportunities to help individuals acquire new skills that will open doors to better employment prospects and financial independence.

Health and Well-being

Our goal is to improve the health and well-being of individuals by providing access to medical care, mental health services, and promoting healthy lifestyles.

Global Impact

We aim to have a global impact by collaborating with like-minded organizations and spreading awareness of the issues affecting vulnerable communities worldwide.

Our Plan

Our roadmap for the future to create lasting change for vulnerable communities.

Short-Term Goals

In the short term, we aim to provide immediate relief to vulnerable families and communities. This includes food distribution, educational support, and access to essential healthcare services.

  • Distribute food and essential supplies
  • Provide educational resources for children
  • Offer healthcare services and medical aid

Medium-Term Goals

In the medium term, our focus will shift to sustainable programs that empower vulnerable groups to become self-sufficient. This includes vocational training, micro-lending programs, and partnerships with local businesses.

  • Launch vocational training programs
  • Start micro-lending and entrepreneurship support
  • Establish local partnerships for community development

Long-Term Goals

Our long-term vision is to create lasting, systemic change in the communities we serve. This includes building infrastructure, creating long-term educational and healthcare access, and advocating for policy changes.

  • Build schools and healthcare facilities
  • Advocate for policy change to protect vulnerable groups
  • Establish long-term sustainability through local ownership

+ What is the mission of the United Family Support Initiative (UFSI)?

The mission of UFSI is to restore dignity to vulnerable groups by reconnecting abandoned children with their families, creating families for orphans and widows, and providing essential support to those in need.

+ How can I contribute to the organization?

You can contribute through monthly donations, volunteering your time, or helping with skills training and capacity-building activities. Contact us for more details.

+ Who does UFSI aim to help?

UFSI focuses on supporting vulnerable groups, including single mothers, orphans, widows, and those who have faced abandonment or rejection.

+ What types of skills training does UFSI offer?

UFSI provides skills training in areas like business development, entrepreneurship, and vocational skills to empower single mothers and young people to become self-reliant.

+ How can I join UFSI as a volunteer?

You can join UFSI as a volunteer by contacting us through our website or social media platforms. We welcome passionate individuals who want to make a difference in the lives of vulnerable groups.

+ What are the key activities of UFSI?

UFSI engages in reconnecting families, providing food and shelter to those in need, organizing skills training, and conducting capacity-building workshops for vulnerable groups.

+ How do I contact UFSI for support or collaboration?

You can contact UFSI through our website's contact form, email, or social media platforms. We are open to partnerships and collaborations to support our mission.
